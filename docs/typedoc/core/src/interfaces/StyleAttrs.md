# Interface: StyleAttrs

Style attributes that can be applied to text.
Used to configure foreground/background colors and text styling.

## Properties

### bg?

> `optional` **bg**: [`ColorInput`](../type-aliases/ColorInput.md)

Background color

***

### blink?

> `optional` **blink**: `boolean`

Blinking text

***

### bold?

> `optional` **bold**: `boolean`

Bold text

***

### dim?

> `optional` **dim**: `boolean`

Dimmed/faint text

***

### fg?

> `optional` **fg**: [`ColorInput`](../type-aliases/ColorInput.md)

Foreground (text) color

***

### italic?

> `optional` **italic**: `boolean`

Italic text

***

### reverse?

> `optional` **reverse**: `boolean`

Reverse video (swap fg/bg colors)

***

### strikethrough?

> `optional` **strikethrough**: `boolean`

Strikethrough text

***

### underline?

> `optional` **underline**: `boolean`

Underlined text
