# Enumeration: RenderableEvents

Events emitted by renderables related to focus state changes.

## Enumeration Members

### BLURRED

> **BLURRED**: `"blurred"`

Fired when the renderable loses focus

***

### FOCUSED

> **FOCUSED**: `"focused"`

Fired when the renderable gains focus
